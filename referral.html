Referrals
Callers may often need resources outside of Harvard Defenders. Below is a list of helpful referrals organized by topic. Please remember to fill out an intake form and save the referral information on Time Matters.

Criminal Justice Institute (CJI)

(617) 496-8143

HLS defense counsel for income-eligible clients beyond the show cause stage.

Committee for Public Counsel Services (CPCS)

(617) 482-6212

Massachusetts public defender service for income-eligible clients beyond the show cause stage.

Youth Advocacy Division

(617) 445-5640 (x8112)

Juvenile defense consultations and representation for income-eligible clients.

Greater Boston Legal Services (GBLS)

(617)371-1234; (800)323-3205

Provide representation on a variety of civil legal issues, including evictions, family law, domestic violence, and government benefits.

Harvard Legal Aid Bureau (HLAB)

(617) 495-4408

HLS civil counsel for income-eligible clients for employment, housing, family law, and other civil legal issues.

Greater Boston Legal Services (GBLS)

(617) 371-1234; (800) 323-3205

Offer representation in CORI sealing hearings.

Prison Legal Assistance Project (PLAP)

(617) 495-3127

Offer legal advice and representation for prisoners in disciplinary hearings and parole revocation hearings.

Massachusetts Prisoners’ Legal Services

(617) 482-2773; 1 (800) 323-3205

Provide advice, referrals, and pro se materials to prisoners and prisoners’ family members on Mondays from 1pm to 4pm. State prisoners may speed dial *9004#. County prisoners may call collect to 617-482-4124.

Boston Bar Association

(617) 742-0625; (800) 552-7046

Massachusetts Bar Association

(617) 654-0400; (866) 627-7577

Mass Legal Services

https://www.masslegalservices.org/findlegalaid

“Clearinghouse” that gives contact information for legal aid programs, nonprofits, government agencies and court programs that may be able to help with legal issues for free or at a low cost.
